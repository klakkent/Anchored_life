About AnchoredLife Church

When you find yourself in a place where you feel hopeless and tired, giving up on your dreams and vision seems like the best solution for you. AnchoredLife partners with you on a journey to rediscovering and transforming your life. Using biblical foundations and tools, we help you discover the fulfilled life that God has ordained for you

“Therefore everyone who hears these words of mine and puts them into practice is like a wise man who built his house on the rock. The rain came down, the streams rose, and the winds blew and beat against that house; yet it did not fall, because it had its foundation on the rock.” Matthew 7:24-25
